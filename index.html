HOME

Patrick M. Villano Elementary School students participate in a comprehensive, developmentally-appropriate and varied academic program. These academic offerings are supported by formal research-based curricular programs and implemented by staff members who have received specific training and professional development to best know these programs. Alongside their in-class academic work, our students take part in a wide variety of school climate and culture activities designed to help them make positive behavior choices as well as be contributing and productive members of the elementary school community.
